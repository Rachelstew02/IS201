The Perfect Playlist

A Curated Mix by Rachel Stewart

Cough Syrup by Young the Giant

The opening to every good playlist needs a song that pulls you in. Something that has you saying: "I Love This Song!"

End of Beginning by Djo

In order to change the pace or tempo of the playlist, you need to include good transitions songs. These songs should keep the vibe but begin to change the pace

Dear John by Taylor Swift

Slow songs are neccessary to a good playlist but they must be emotional.

Rocketeer by Far East Movement & Ryan Tedder

Another example of a transition song. Big and smooth like the previous song but transitioning genres into Throwbacks.

Boyfriend by Justin Bieber

The perfect playlist should be a one that everyone can sing along to. This is why throwbacks are a must!

Before He Cheats by Carrie Underwood

Big emotions are always welcome.

Two Black Cadillacs by Carrie Underwood

Never shy away from including multiple songs from an artists repetoire.

No Body No Crime by Taylor Swift & HAIM

This transition song takes the theme and emotions of the previous song and changes the tempo to prepare for the final song.

Saturn Live by SZA

The finale of a playlist is just as important as the beginning. This song should mark the listener and keep them thinking even after the song is done.
